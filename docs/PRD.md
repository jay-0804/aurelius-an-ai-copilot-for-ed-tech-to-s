# Product Requirements Document (PRD): AI Copilot for Ed Tech Helpdesk
===========================================================

## Vision
--------

Our vision is to revolutionize the ed tech support experience by introducing an AI-powered copilot that assists helpdesk teams in providing efficient, personalized, and timely support to students, teachers, and administrators. This copilot will leverage artificial intelligence to automate routine tasks, provide instant answers, and enable helpdesk teams to focus on complex issues that require human expertise.

## Problem
--------

The current ed tech helpdesk experience is often plagued by:

* Long wait times for support requests
* Inadequate or incomplete solutions to common issues
* Insufficient resources and training for helpdesk teams
* High volume of repetitive and routine support requests

These pain points lead to frustration, decreased productivity, and a negative overall experience for students, teachers, and administrators.

## Solution
---------

Our AI copilot for ed tech helpdesk will address these challenges by:

* Providing instant answers to common support questions through a knowledge base and chat interface
* Automating routine tasks such as password resets, account unlocks, and basic troubleshooting
* Analyzing support requests to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement
* Offering personalized recommendations and solutions based on user behavior, preferences, and learning styles
* Enabling helpdesk teams to focus on complex, high-touch issues that require human expertise and empathy

## Target Users
--------------

Our primary target users are:

* Students (K-12 and higher education)
* Teachers and instructors
* Administrators and IT staff
* Helpdesk teams and support specialists

## MVP Features
--------------

The minimum viable product (MVP) will include the following features:

1. **Knowledge Base**: A comprehensive, searchable database of common support questions and answers
2. **Chat Interface**: A user-friendly chat interface that allows users to ask questions and receive instant answers
3. **Automation**: Automated workflows for routine tasks such as password resets and account unlocks
4. **Analytics**: Basic analytics and reporting to track support requests, response times, and user engagement
5. **Integration**: Integration with popular ed tech platforms and learning management systems (LMS)

## Metrics
--------

We will measure the success of our AI copilot through the following key performance indicators (KPIs):

1. **Response Time**: Average time to respond to support requests
2. **Resolution Rate**: Percentage of support requests resolved on the first contact
3. **User Satisfaction**: Overall user satisfaction with the support experience (measured through surveys and feedback)
4. **Automation Rate**: Percentage of routine tasks automated through the AI copilot
5. **Adoption Rate**: Percentage of users who adopt and regularly use the AI copilot

## Roadmap
---------

Our roadmap for the AI copilot includes the following milestones:

1. **Research and Development** (Weeks 1-4): Conduct market research, gather user feedback, and develop the MVP
2. **Pilot Launch** (Weeks 5-8): Launch the MVP with a small group of pilot users and gather feedback
3. **Iteration and Refining** (Weeks 9-12): Refine the AI copilot based on user feedback and iterate on new features
4. **Full Launch** (Weeks 13-16): Launch the AI copilot to the wider ed tech community
5. **Ongoing Development** (After Week 16): Continuously gather user feedback, iterate on new features, and improve the AI copilot

## User Stories
--------------

The following user stories outline the key functionality and user experience of the AI copilot:

1. **As a student, I want to be able to ask questions and receive instant answers through a chat interface, so I can quickly resolve common issues and get back to learning.**
2. **As a teacher, I want to be able to access a comprehensive knowledge base and receive personalized recommendations, so I can provide better support to my students and improve their learning outcomes.**
3. **As a helpdesk team member, I want to be able to automate routine tasks and focus on complex, high-touch issues, so I can provide more efficient and effective support to users.**
4. **As an administrator, I want to be able to track support requests, response times, and user engagement, so I can identify areas for improvement and optimize our support operations.**
5. **As a user, I want to be able to provide feedback and suggestions, so I can help improve the AI copilot and ensure it meets my needs and expectations.**
